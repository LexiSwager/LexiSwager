Fujifilm TX-1

The Fujifilm TX-1 is a interchangeable lens 35mm panoramic rangefinder camera released in 1998. It was manufactured by Fujifilm as a joint project with Swedish camera maker Hasselblad who markets the camera under the branding XPan. The body is made from titanium and aluminium. The back of the camera is covered by a rubberized material and has a good thumb grip for the right hand. TX-1 are common in a silver colour, while the XPan in a painted black. Wood and rubberized front hand grips have been produced, and are often swapped with one another. Home made and third party wood grips have also been manufactured.
