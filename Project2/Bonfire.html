The flames of the fire start dropping, soon to go out. Suddenly there is a rustling in the woods followed but a low growls.

“Don’t panic!”, yells a counselor, “it’s probably a bear that smelled our food. The best thing we can do is stay as a group and walk back to the cabins”. But you’re so scared, you know its not really a bear! How can the counselor's tell you to keep calm? Should you run or stay in the group?
