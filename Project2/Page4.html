“I swear it’s real!” Cries a boy who looks like he’s about to break down in tears. He can’t be older than ten years old.

Another boy beside him (possibly brothers) is a lot calmer. “It’s true, I’ve hear it howling at night”. His younger brother looks up at him graciously through tears.

“I don’t believe it“, remarks one of the campers. “I need proof!”

The eldest brother speaks again, “why don’t I tell you a story instead. The legend of the monster in Grover Camp”.
